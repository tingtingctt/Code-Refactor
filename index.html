Guard Dog (DVD)

This is the movie that OES Rescue dogs Garth and Norman filmed in Las Vegas during the summer, 2014.

Garth had his ears dyed black. Much of his work was left on the editing floor and he had a stand in (thanks Norman). Rory Johnston and Lance Burton (magicians in Vegas) were the main stays.

Guard Dog is a nice family story about a nine-year old boy who is lying, cheating, stealing and flunking our of school. Then, one night, a talking, magical Old English Sheepdog (Garth and Norman!) appears and promises to make his dreams come true, if he can follow “The Rules”.
